Quick, Easy Mochi

Description

A traditional japanase dish, this fast mochi serves a delightful snack. Have a taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 cuo sweetened red bean paste.
  • 1 cup glutinous rice flour.
  • 1 teaspoon green tea powder (matcha).
  • 1 cup water.
  • 0.25 cup white sugar.
  • 0.5 cup cornstarch.
