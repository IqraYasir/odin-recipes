Chana Masala (Savoury Indian Chickpeas)

Description

Give this masala dish a try. It is full of flavour and still features a good range of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 chopped onion.
  • 1 chopped tomato.
  • 1 inch of fresh ginger, peeled and chopped.
  • 4 cloves garlic, chopped.
  • (Optional) 1 green chile pepper, seeded and chopped.
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil.
  • 2 fresh bay leaves.
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder.
  • 1 teaspoon corainder powder.
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala.
  • 0.5 teaspoon turmeric powder.
  • A pinch of salt.
  • Water as needed.
  • 15 ounces of chickpeas.
  • 1 teaspoon fresh cilantro leaves.
